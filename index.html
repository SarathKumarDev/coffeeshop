what Makes Our Coffee Special

When the beans are picked and dried, they end up in a variety of sizes or grades. These boutique companies roast all beans of varying size together because they cannot afford to waste any beans regardless of quality and they don’t have enough beans for it to make sense to separate them into different grades.

What you end up with is an inconsistent roast – the larger beans might not be roasted enough, while the smaller beans are roasted too much and taste burnt.

Our “Estate” coffee is from a single grade so you get a much more uniform roast at the perfect temperature for that size.
